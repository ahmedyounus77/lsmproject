About the Qasimi Industries (Pvt) Ltd

Qasimi Industries (Pvt) Ltd is a leading enterprise that has earned its reputation among the international and domestic buyers by producing the high quality synthetic fabric. The company was established in 1994 and within the span of two decades its products gradually won the major market share and gained unprecedented success by becoming the top choice for its customers.
